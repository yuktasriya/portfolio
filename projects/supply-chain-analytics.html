Supply Chain Analytics (Tableau)

Tableau · LOD · YOY/YTD · Performance Tuning

Problem

Supply chain stakeholders needed interactive views for trends, rankings, and exceptions across large datasets.

Impact
  • Improved dashboard performance and usability for day-to-day operational analysis.
  • Increased adoption via clear KPI storytelling and training sessions.
Approach
  • Built Tableau dashboards using LOD expressions for consistent aggregation.
  • Added YOY/YTD metrics and dynamic rankings to spotlight performance drivers.
  • Optimized extracts/filters and reduced heavy calculations on high-cardinality fields.
  • Conducted stakeholder walkthroughs and iterated based on feedback.
