Healthcare Operations Scorecards (Tableau + Informatica)

Tableau · Informatica · Data Profiling · UAT

Problem

Operations teams needed daily scorecards from multiple legacy systems with strong validation and clear definitions.

Impact
  • Improved trust in reporting by documenting definitions and fixing root-cause data issues.
  • Enabled faster operational decisions with standardized scorecards.
Approach
  • Integrated sources via Informatica and created curated tables with business-rule validations.
  • Delivered Tableau scorecards with calculated fields and KPI thresholds.
  • Created STTM mapping, data dictionaries, and lineage notes to support auditability.
  • Supported UAT by triaging issues, tracing to source, and implementing fixes with regression checks.
